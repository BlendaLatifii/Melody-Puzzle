Stealth is a popular singer. Real name Fatjon Dibra. In 1998, he immigrated to England at the age of 6. Lives in East London. Stealth has a son and is part of the Hellbanianz group. On November 19, 2016, Stealth releases the video clip "This Is Style". On December 18, 2016, Vinz releases the video clip "Nobody", in collaboration with Stealth. On February 10, 2017, Stealth releases the video clip "Nuk Ka Dashni", in collaboration with Vinz. On July 28, 2017, Vinz releases the video clip "Hood Life", in collaboration with Stealth. On October 21, 2017, Vinz releases the video clip "Bojn Demek", in collaboration with Stealth. On March 26, 2018, Stealth releases the video clip "Per Shokun I Veshi Dorezat", in collaboration with Vinz. On May 7, 2018, Stealth releases the music video "Never Late", in collaboration with Vinz. Stealth has announced that it will soon release the video clip "Gango", in collaboration with Cozman & Vinz.
