BUTRINT IMERI

  • Birthplace:
  • Lorrach
  • Lives:
  • Germany
  • Age:
  • 27
  • Birthdate:

Butrint Imeri is a very popular singer and songwriter. After many successes in Germany and Europe, Butrinti is presented to the Albanian public with the super-hit "Ki Me Lyp". With the tireless work, talent, and great love that Butrinti has for music, he brings us a complete and professional singer, who knows how to sing and complete his presentation with a decent appearance, professional dance, that does not we have seen it so far on our stage. He has talent, he has unique style, expressive movements, an amazing voice! He is the new star of Albanian music and he gives love to music, as it seems his dream to debut in Kosovo has finally come true. Born in Germany, Butrint grew up with the sounds of music. Extremely talented, with a passion and love for music, with a pleasant and special voice, and a wonderful appearance, he completed his skills with music studies in Lorrach, Germany, at the prestigious pop music academy "Voice Lap", founded by the famous German artist Xavier Naidoo. Also, since he was 11 years old, he was part of the dance academy "Dance Energy", where for 7 years in a row he managed to rise professionally and appear in many cities in Germany and beyond. The awards and evaluations were countless for the young Albanian talent, who was just now creating his style and personality as a singer and dancer. One of the biggest performances was his appearance as a dancer in the "Dance Camp" of Germany, where he managed to triumph, among the best dancers from all over the world.
