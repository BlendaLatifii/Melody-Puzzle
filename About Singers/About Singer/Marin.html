Marin is a well-known singer and songwriter. His name is Maringlen Malaj from Has and he lives in Has. He started his career at a young age and introduced you to the path of hip hop and has the talent and desire that unites him. He is 25 years old. He had a very good music project, the song Xhan which was released by the REDBOX Entertainment studio, which it was a pretty good success for Marini. He participated in the Shkodra Fest festival, which won with the song Kingin in 2014. Now he currently works alone. On May 7, 2014, TnT released the music video "Chill Up In The Sky", in collaboration with Dieter & Demoone. On June 13, 2014, TnT released the music video "Mos U Kthe", in collaboration with Dieter. On October 17, 2014, TnT released the music video for "On The Phone". In December 2014, TnT participates in Shkodra Fest 1 with the song "Kingin". In January 2015, TnT participates in Prizreni Music Fest 2015 with the song "Where The Limit". In January 2016, TnT participates in Shkodra Fest 2 with the song "Barz Room". On June 28, 2016, Marin released the video clip "Me Fal". On October 30, 2016, Marini released the video clip "Xhan". The music of the song was composed by Big Bang, the lyrics were written by Marin TnT. On August 4, 2017, Marin releases the music video "Tonight". On August 26, 2019, Marin released the video clip "£Hasjanali". On October 18, 2019, Marin releases the music video "£Pr-Tr". The orchestration of the song was done by Marin TnT. The video clip was produced by the company MAX Production. On December 12, 2019, Marin releases the music video "£Poundz". On March 1, 2020, Marin releases the video clip "Hala N'rrug".
