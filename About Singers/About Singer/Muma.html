Endrit Mumajesi is a well-known songwriter and singer. Endrit Mumajesi is one of the most talented authors of art. He was born in Tirana. He completed primary school at the "Ali Demi" school in Tirana, while secondary school at the "Sinan Tafaj" school. He started his journey with music at the age of 15. The first song he collaborated with and wrote the lyrics for is the song "A life with you" by Vedat Ademi. He has also written several well-known texts and is currently part of the ThreeDots production. On October 23, 2016, Genta Ismajli released the video clip "Squat Baby", in collaboration with Muma. The music of the song was composed by Alfred Sula, the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi. The music video was produced by Entermedia. On November 3, 2017, Fjolla Morina & Muma release the video clip "Culo". The music of the song was composed by Alfred Sula, the text was written by Endrit Mumajesi, while Alfred Sula took care of the orchestration. On April 21, 2021, Cricket & Muma release the music video "Without You". The music of the song was composed by Cricket & Endrit Mumajesi, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi. Audio production was done by Cricket. On June 6, 2021, Cricket, Muma & Dafina Zeqiri release the music video "Pa Ty (Remix)". The music of the song was composed by Cricket & Endrit Mumajesi, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi. Audio production was done by Cricket. On September 29, 2021, Muma & Elinel release the music video "Per Tradhetine". The music of the song was composed by Cricket, Endrit Mumajesi & Elinel, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi & Elinel. Audio production was done by Cricket. On October 22, 2021, Cricket & Muma release the music video "Theme". The music of the song was composed by Cricket & Endrit Mumajesi, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi. The music video was produced by Imagine Films, while the audio production was done by Cricket. On March 7, 2022, Dafina Zeqiri releases the video clip "Love", in collaboration with Muma. The music of the song was composed by Cricket, Endrit Mumajesi & Dafina Zeqiri, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi & Elinel. The music video was produced by Entermedia, while the audio production was done by Cricket. On July 8, 2022, Lumi B publishes the video clip "Nale Nale", in collaboration with Muma. The song was composed by Cricket, while the lyrics were written together by Lumi B & Endrit Mumajesi. The music video was produced by Imagine Films, while the audio production was done by Cricket. On December 13, 2022, Muma & Cricket release the music video "Ca Mbone". The music of the song comes as a collaboration between Cricket & Endrit Mumajesi, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi himself. Audio production was done by Cricket. On November 10, 2023, Muma & Teuta Selimi release the video clip "U Deshtëm". The music of the song comes as a collaboration between Cricket & Endrit Mumajesi, while the lyrics were written by Endrit Mumajesi himself. The music video was produced by Black Fish Entertainment, while the audio production was done by Cricket.
